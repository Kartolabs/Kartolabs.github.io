About Me

Download CV

I am a Lecturer at Lupane State University, Zimbabwe. I teach courses such as Introduction to GIS and Remote sensing, Principles of GIS, Climatology and Meteorology, Environmental Impact Assesment, Research Methods in Geography and Land degradation and Reclamation. I also supervise over 7 Undegraduate students' projects. I hold a first class masters degree in Cartography and Geoinformatics from Perm State University in Russia and a first class bachelors degree in Geography and Population Studies from Lupane State University, Zimbabwe. My interests are ecological restoration, biodiversity conservation and climate change. My masters reseach focused on understating the spatiotemporal dynamics of soil erosion risk in Umzingwane catchment area and its implications for Integrated water resources management (IWRM). Im affluent in GIS programmes such as Google Earth Engine, Agisoft Metashape, ArcMap Desktop, ESRI products, QGIS, MAPINFO, ENVI, Easy trace, Geoserver, PostGIS, Microsoft Office and Blender. I also work with programming languages such as python, Java, SQL, HTML and CSS.
