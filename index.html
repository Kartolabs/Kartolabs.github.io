About Me

I am an Msc Cartography and Geoinformatics student at Perm State University in Russia. My interests are cartography, geoinformatics, artifficial intelligence and machine learning, soil erosion and Sustainable conservation My masters reseach focused on understating the spatiotemporal dynamics of soil erosion risk in Umzingwane catchment area and its implications for Integrated water resources management (IWRM). Im affluent in GIS programmes such as Google Earth Engine, Agisoft Metashape, ArcMap Desktop, ESRI products, QGIS, MAPINFO, ENVI, Easy trace, Geoserver, PostGIS, Microsoft Office and Blender. I also work with programming languages such as python, Java, SQL, HTML and CSS.
